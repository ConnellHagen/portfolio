Firefly Chemotaxis

I used fireflies as a way to demonstrate chemotaxis, which is a natural process of diffusion where random movements following a general pattern eventually reach an end state. The fireflies themselves light up like in real life, and I made sure to desync them so it doesn't look weird. The fireflies also follow the mouse which was tough to code in a realistic way, as all of the fireflies moving in the exact same way together looks very unnatural. Another problem was that once all of the fireflies crowded around the mouse, they wouldn't move away. I fixed this by making fireflies have a small chance of moving very far away at once, and by making them move at different speeds when following the mouse.
